     MAKING TESTS:
     All tests are d100 rolls, trying to roll under or equal to a target number. The target number is determined
     by your skill rating, with modiﬁers applied depending on how difﬁcult the task is. 00 is always a success;
     99 always a failure. ■ p. 115
